Warpaffine

by

  • Audience:

This is an experimental effort at implementing a “Deskew operation” with the best performance possible.

With an acquisition from a Lattice Lightsheet microscope the image slices are created at a skewed angle. In order to create a regular volumetric image in Cartesian coordinates, the volume needs to undergo a geometric transformation (and affine transformation, hence the name of this project) and needs to be resampled.
